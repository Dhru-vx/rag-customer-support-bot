COMPANY OVERVIEW:
We are an online e-commerce platform providing electronics, fashion, home appliances, and accessories across India.

RETURN POLICY:
• Customers can return products within 7 days of delivery.
• Product must be unused and in original packaging.
• Invoice must be provided for return.
• Some categories like innerwear and personal care items are non-returnable.
• Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after quality check.

REPLACEMENT POLICY:
• Replacement is allowed for damaged or defective products.
• Customers must upload images of damaged product within 48 hours.
• Replacement is subject to stock availability.

CANCELLATION POLICY:
• Orders can be cancelled before shipment.
• Once shipped, cancellation is not possible.
• Prepaid cancellations are refunded within 3-5 days.
• COD orders cannot be cancelled after dispatch.

WARRANTY POLICY:
• Electronics have 1-year manufacturer warranty.
• Extended warranty available for select products.
• Physical damage and water damage are not covered.
• Warranty claims require original invoice.

SHIPPING POLICY:
• Standard shipping: 3-5 business days.
• Express shipping: 1-2 business days.
• Remote areas may take 7-10 days.
• Shipping charges may apply for orders below ₹499.

REFUND POLICY:
• Refunds are credited to original payment method.
• COD refunds are processed to bank account.
• Refund timeline: 5-7 working days.
• Bank processing time may vary.

PAYMENT POLICY:
• We accept UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking.
• EMI available for select credit cards.
• Failed payments are automatically refunded within 24 hours.

ACCOUNT & SECURITY POLICY:
• Customers must maintain account confidentiality.
• Suspicious activity may result in temporary suspension.
• Password reset available via registered email.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT:
• Support available 9 AM – 9 PM IST.
• Support channels: Chat, Email, Phone.
• Response time: Within 24 hours.
